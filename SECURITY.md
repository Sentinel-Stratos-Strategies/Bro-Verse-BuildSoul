Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly.

How to report:
Email us at security@yourdomain.com with details of the issue and steps to reproduce it.

Confirmation:
You will receive an acknowledgment within 48 hours confirming that your report was received.

Response timeline:
We aim to provide an initial assessment within 5 business days and will work with you on a fix, if verified.

Disclosure:
Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have released an official fix and given approval to share details.

Your responsible disclosure helps keep the community safe and secure. Thank you for your cooperation and vigilance.
